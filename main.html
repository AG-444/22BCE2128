VITEATS - A food review website for vitians

  • Find places to eat in or outside VIT
  • Review food for your favourite restuarants
  • Discover new items on Menus

We are aiming to create a website to solve all food problems of VITians.Some of are goals are:

  • to include all restaurants in and outside VIT that students frequent.
  • to get menus of the restaurants
  • to get reviews of dishes from students
  • create ability to search through restaurants by filtering based on different criteria
  • an weekly updated list best food
